Why I'm Excited About This Job

Collaborating daily with great people for the betterment of the end-user.
Coming from a strong customer service background, I care deeply about the end-user experience. Vocationally speaking, I have spent the last six years of my life championing the cause of the customer. I want to make products that customers enjoy using, and I want to be part of a team that loves what they do. Code42's core value of "best idea wins" promotes a healthy environment for the exchange of ideas, with the ultimate goal of creating the best products possible.

The opportunity to make things I'm proud of.
Code42 already has an outstanding web presence. The rebranding and relaunch of the consumer CrashPlan properties last year were inspiring to see. Contributing to a high-talent, high-performance team that's already creating gorgeous and functional sites would be incredibly energizing. There is still work to be done; aligning the PRO and PROe web properties with current branding, and continuing to be a leader in our industry is something I want to be a part of.

I enjoy working hard and learning new things.
Being a highly self-motivated person, I'm not afraid of a challenge. A job that doesn't push you to your limits and stretch your capabilities isn't worth having. I program because I enjoy it, and am frequently and intentionally exposing myself to new things, not simply doing enough to get by. I want to be challenged and inspired every day.
